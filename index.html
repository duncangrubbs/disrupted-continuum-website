Welcome to the official website for Disrupted Continumm

Disrupted Continuum is an independent grunge/hard rock/punk from the San Francisco bay area. The four members of the band are Leila Siegel-Zigmund (Vocals), Cole Bailey (Drums), Noa Zimmerman (Bass), and Gianni Pompili (Lead guitar).

Leila Siegel-Zigmund has been playing guitar for almost four years, recording a solo EP called Toxic Storm before starting Disrupted Continuum. If you would like to know more about each of the members, check out the about page.
