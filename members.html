Leila Siegel-Zigmund is the vocalist and leader of Disrupted Continuum. She grew up just north of San Francisco in Marin County where she currently lives. She is a junior at Sir Francis Drake High School. She started playing guitar right before freshman year and has been completely devoted ever since. Before she started playing guitar she played piano. She has a lot of experience with music and is a very talented guitar player.
